Shadow Containment Zone
A forgotten containment facility… buried deep beneath the surface where no one dares to go.
This place was once a secret research center built to control “shadow entities.” But after a catastrophic failure, the entire system collapsed—leaving behind only dim lights, malfunctioning machines, and shadows that seem to move on their own.
Inside, you’ll find narrow corridors, abandoned laboratories, heavy steel doors that open and close by themselves, and areas consumed by total darkness…
The deeper you go, the more “alive” the shadows become.
Those who enter begin to hear whispers… see illusions… and some simply vanish without a trace.
No one truly knows what “it” is.
All we know… is that it doesn’t want anyone to leave.
Your Objective:
Survive, uncover the truth, and escape the containment zone… before the shadows consume you.
